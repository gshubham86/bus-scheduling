University Bus Mangement System
Chitkara University, Punjab has made secure and safe arrangement of transport facility for students, staff & faculty. The transport facility is available from Chandigarh, Mohali, Kharar, Patiala, Derabassi, Panchkula, Ambala, Kalka, Zirakpur, Landran, Rajpura, Banur, Khanna, Sirhind, Doraha and Ludhina. All the buses reach the campus by 8:45 am (Approximately) and leave the Campus by 4:45 pm (Approximately) or as per scheduled activities / events in the University.
